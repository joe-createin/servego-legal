ServeGo – Privacy Policy

Last updated:

ServeGo collects only the data necessary to operate the Provider app, such as account details, booking information, and diagnostics to improve reliability. We do not sell personal data.

Data we collect

  • Account info: phone/email, name, profile, service categories.
  • Bookings & chat: messages and booking details for customer support and service delivery.
  • Location (optional): used for service areas and navigation. You can disable it in device settings.
  • Notifications: device tokens are used to send booking updates.
  • Analytics & diagnostics: crash and performance data to improve app quality.

How we use data

  • Provide core features (bookings, chat, earnings).
  • Send important updates (notifications about requests and changes).
  • Prevent abuse and maintain service reliability.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing may occur with service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) strictly to operate the app, under data processing agreements.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law.

Your choices

  • Disable location permissions in your device settings at any time.
  • Opt out of notifications in device settings.
  • Request data access or deletion by contacting us.
