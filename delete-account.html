ServeGo Provider — Account Deletion

This page explains how ServeGo Provider users (service providers) can request deletion of their account and personal data.

How to request deletion

  1. In-app: Go to Profile → Account → Delete account (if available)
  2. Email: Send a request from your registered email to support@servego.app
    Subject: Delete my ServeGo Provider account
    Include: your name, registered email/phone, and (optional) reason.

We’ll verify ownership before proceeding.

What we delete

  • Profile & identity details
  • Login credentials
  • Uploaded media (portfolio photos, documents)
  • In-app preferences & push notification tokens
  • Chats/messages (deleted or anonymized where feasible)

What we may retain

  • Payment/tax/transaction records
  • Fraud-prevention & security logs
  • Legal dispute records

Retained data is access-restricted and deleted once no longer required.

Timeline

  • Acknowledge within 3 business days
  • Delete/anonymize within 7–30 days
  • Backups may persist up to 90 days

Third-party services

We use Firebase (Auth, Firestore/Storage, FCM) and Google Sign-In. We remove or anonymize your data with these providers in line with this policy.

Contact: support@servego.app
